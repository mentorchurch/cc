Developer Updates & Comments:

Good news! as of 3/25/2026, we’ve added more hymns—with additional selections on the way.
We’ve also introduced a new announcement clip that clearly explains the purpose of the station,
what it offers, and why it was created.

Recent site updates now include built-in audio processing designed to improve overall sound quality.
When active, the site will display: ENHANCED AUDIO ENABLED.
Otherwise, it will show: STANDARD AUDIO ONLY.

Due to operating system restrictions on mobile devices (phones and tablets),
enhanced audio will not function there—only standard playback is supported.
Full enhanced audio capability is available on desktop systems, including PCs and Macs,
where browser limitations are less restrictive.

This project has been a true labor of love, and we are honored to share it.
We remain committed to expanding the hymn library and improving the listening experience
over time. Your support and patience as we continue to refine the site are greatly appreciated.
