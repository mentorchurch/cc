The making of devotional radio
The goal of this site is rather simple: honor God
by presenting His word and hymns accessible to anyone who
wishes to listen or read. All hymns were recorded
during worship services at the Mentor Oh Church of Christ.

Our site mixes live hymns, spoken scriptures and enviromental
sounds of God's creation. The site is a gift to the church, 500+ lines of
code and about 78 hours of work all thrown in the collection plate!
We use a plain design—dark background, simple text, and no imagery.
Why? because the goal isn’t decoration. The goal is to let the message
carry the weight, not the presentation. The structure of hymns,
scripture readings, and enviromental sounds follows a very old pattern
of worship. First century Christian gatherings centered around
reading scripture, prayer and singing. The site is basically
trying to recreate that experience in a digital form.

When you visit our site, the script counts every file that has been uploaded.
It then creates a randomized playlist and starts playing. If you reload the site,
a new random playlist will be created. It is designed to be
infinitely scalable and can support thousands of hymns.
As we continue attending services, we will continue recording and uploading new material.

Jesus is Lord!
